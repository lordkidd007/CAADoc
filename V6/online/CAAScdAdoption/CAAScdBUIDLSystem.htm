System

This table summarizes the V5 System Automation objects, properties, and methods that you may have used in your applications built on top of V5 and that do not exist any longer with V6. For each of these:

  • If the object still exists as in V5, but in a different framework, its V6 typelib is given.
  • If a one-to-one mapping exists to replacing objects, properties, or methods, links to these substitutes are listed and pointed to
  • Otherwise, an adoption article is pointed to to explain how to replace or get rid of them.
