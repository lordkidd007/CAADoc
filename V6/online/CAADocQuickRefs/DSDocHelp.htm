Encyclopedia

This section briefly describes the various aids provided to help you find the information you need quickly and efficiently as well as navigate easily in the Encyclopedia.

Full-text search
Use the Search box in the banner to search for specific terms or phrases. See Search Help.
Main topics bar
Expand and collapse nodes in the table of contents on the left side of the Encyclopedia portal to move quickly from one section to another. The Encyclopedia is organized by programming languages, then by compass quadrants, along with an Common Services section. Then you can find a Development Tools section, a Rules and Guides section, a References, and an Installation section.
Topic navigation
Breadcrumb trail
Use the breadcrumb trail located at the top of the content area of each page to know where you are in the guide and to go directly to a higher level.
Navigation box
Use the References section at the bottom of each page, or the Related Topics box at the top right to navigate to related use cases, concepts, understanding, how-to, and reference information.
Inline cross-references
Use these links to move from one part of a page to another part of the same page.

All these aids are designed to help you know where you are and how the Encyclopedia is organized as well as to give you a sense of context.

Native Apps Development Toolset User Assistance

Different forms of online user assistance are available to the end-user when working with the Native Apps Development Toolset. Seamlessly integrated into the overall design, they enable you to get help easily. See Getting Help.
