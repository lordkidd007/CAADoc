Adding this Page to Your Favorites

Security rules prevent from automatically adding this page to your favorites, but you can nevertheless add it to your favorites and save its navigation context as follows:

  1. On the Favorites menu, click Add to Favorites....

  2. Copy the name below and paste it into the Name box:

  3. Click Add.

  4. On the Favorites menu, click Organize Favorites....

  5. Locate the newly added favorite.

  6. Right click the favorite just added, and click Properties.

  7. Copy the URL below and paste it to replace the one in the URL box:

  8. Click OK.

  9. In the Organize Favorites... dialog box, click Close.

This favorite now opens this page in its navigation context.
