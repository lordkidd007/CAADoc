Material Modeler Architecture
Technical Article

Abstract

This article presents an overview of the Material Architecture.

  • Objectives
  • Architecture

Objectives

The objectives of the Material Modeler are multiple:

  1. Provide an infrastructure for all applicative domains.

    We create a Representation in which you will be able to store your applicative domain feature.

  2. Provide an architecture to be able to support Core and Covering Materials.
  3. Provide Tools and Interfaces to interact with the Material Model.

    API have been provided to navigate on the Material Modeler, to get and retrieve Applicative domain, to apply material on data, etc.

  4. Enable to classify the Material Reference in Folders.

Architecture

A material is made of a Material Reference. It can aggregate several Applicative Domains which are modeled as Instance of Representation.

Example of the Data Model of an Aluminum Reference with Three Applicative Domains

Focus on the internal structure of a representation

The provided representation is managed by a root feature which inherits from CATMaterialRepManager. Your applicative feature for your domain will be aggregated under this root manager.

Focus on the architecture of the Apply Material

For both types of Core and Covering Material, the Apply Material is modeled by a connection pointed at the material support and at the Material Reference.
