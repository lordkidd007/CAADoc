VPM Enterprise Environment Customization

This guide deals with customizing data models and behaviors of PLM objects.

Warning: Except for "Pre-customized" modelers, any customization is for demonstration purposes, and must not be used for production deployment

As such, most of VPM Customization samples illustrating this guide, as delivered through the VPM Environment, are not relevant to be put in customer production. It may be used for demonstration purpose only, and may change over the time to better illustrate customization capabilities.

OOTB Team Customization, as delivered through the PLM_Team Environment, is relevant to be put as-is in customer production. This OOTB Team Customization is not mentioned in this guide and is not a customization starter. It may be used for production, given the fact it is not modified by the customer. OOTB Customization may evolve over the time to better match customer and end user expectations.

Administrators need to choose the OOTB Team Customization for production, or engineers implement and deploy their own customizations for production. Several customization techniques need to be put in action in order to achieve a customization, starting from the data model customization on server up to Business Logic integration on Rich client. The purpose of this guide is to focus on Customizing the VPM Modelers.
