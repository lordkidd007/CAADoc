ENOVIA Studio News

The framework IdentityCard files are now encoded using XML, and the C++ header format is deprecated. Both are supported in V6R2012x. Refer to The Framework Identity Card section to know more about XML IdentityCard files, and specially to Migrating Your Identity Cards to XML to change your framework IdentityCard files to XML.

Encyclopedia News
