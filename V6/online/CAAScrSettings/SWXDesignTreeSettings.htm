SWXDesignTreeSettings

Role: Settings to customize appearance of Design Tree

The SWXDesignTreeSettings repository has the following characteristics:

  • Primary Tab Page: /Mechanical Design/SolidWorks V6/Design Tree
  • Repository File: SWXDesignTreeSettings
  • Version: 1
  • Full Compatibility: 1
  • Upward Compatibility: 1
