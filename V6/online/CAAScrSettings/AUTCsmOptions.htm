AUTCsmOptions

Role: Generic Settings for Automation Workbenches

Detailed role:Generic Settings for Automation Workbenches

The AUTCsmOptions repository has the following characteristics:

  • Primary Tab Page: /Automation/General
  • Repository File: AUTCsmOptions
  • Version: 1
  • Full Compatibility: 1
  • Upward Compatibility: 1
  • Setting controller:
    • Framework_CPP: AUTCsmUI
    • Framework_IDL: AUTCsmUI
    • Interface_CPP: AUTICsmGeneralSettingAtt
    • Interface_IDL: AUTICsmGeneralSettingAtt
    • LateType: CATSysSettingCtrl
