Weight and Center of Gravity Modeler Overview

Weight and Center of Gravity offers designers a simple and intuitive interface to retrieve the computed weight, or to define the declared weight, of the different parts that make up an assembly. Furthermore, Weight and Center of Gravity enables weight managers to check the global weight of assemblies, know precisely how the weight is distributed within the assembly, as well as track parts for which no weight has been computed or declared. In close correlation with the bill of material view, Weight and Center of Gravity manages both CAD and BOM data, thus offering tight integration with other applications such as Piping and Tubing, Insulation, Electrical or Mechanical.

Licensing

  • Weight Modeler API in the Authoring layer are available if the technical product WCG license is granted. WCG is included in TDD (CATIA 3D Design) or IDE (CATIA Industrial Design) commercial products.
  • Weight Modeler API in the Navigation layer are available if the commercial product LWG (CATIA Live Weight) license is granted.
