Abstract

This article describes the public contents of the MechMod catalog. It lists the StartUps and the attributes of each that are available for consulting, as well as their derivability authorizations. The "Integrating a New Mechanical Feature" technical article describes how to create a new startup deriving from those of the MechMod catalog and how to integrate them correctly in the application.
