This section deals with the PLM modelers you can access from your apps thanks to programming interfaces.

Some of these modelers can be gathered to address paradigms, such as:

  • RFLP for Systems Engineering:
    • Requirements
    • Functional
    • Logical
    • Physical, named here the Product modeler.
  • PPRS for Manufacturing Engineering:
    • Product
    • Process
    • Resource
    • System
  • MSR: Model Scenario Result for both Manufacturing and Simulation Engineering.
