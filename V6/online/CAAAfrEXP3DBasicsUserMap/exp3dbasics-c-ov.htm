Overview
 

The IFWE Compass User Experience Guide is intended for users who need to become quickly familiar with the key concepts and capabilities of the 3DEXPERIENCE Platform and the IFWE Compass.

The IFWE Compass User Experience provides a unique visual signature as well as a clear and unified access to key services for all 3DEXPERIENCE Platform apps. It also applies a brand new graphic charter that is fully compliant with touch devices.

You can learn more about it in the following sections:

  • Compass
  • Top Bar
  • 6WTags
  • Tabs
  • Action Bar.

(1) Compass
The door to all 3DEXPERIENCE Platform apps and experiences.
(2) Top bar
Enables you to access the following key services:

(a) Search: search for content

(b) 6WTags: reveal content semantics and capture your own

(c) Me: access user-related information

(d) Add: create and add content

(e) Share: share, save and collaborate

(f) Home: switch context between your communities and 3DSpace

(g) Help: access user assistance and the support community.

(3) Tabs
Enable you to organize and navigate your content.
(4) Action bar
Lets you access all actions within an app. The action bar is organized in sections.

For more information about commands common to all apps, see Native Apps Common Services and 3D Modeling Apps Common Services.
