Search

Lets you search across the 3DEXPERIENCE Platform or within the current tab.

The search scope can be set in the drop-down menu at the left of the box. This allows you to define WHAT to search, WHERE to search and HOW to search.

Search results can be retrieved using 6WTags.

6WTags

Click or tap on the tag icon next to the search box to reveal the 6WTags.

6WTags let you capture the semantic on any content (either automatically extracted, or created by users).

Tags can then be used to refine search results or any content list. They follow the 6W classification: Who, When, What, Where, Why, How. For more information, see 6WTags.

Me Services

Lets you access any user-related information such as: Profile, Preferences or Notifications.

Its content may vary depending on the current app.

Add Services

Lets you access new content creation such as: Post, Task, Part, Simulation or Process.

Its content may vary depending on the current app.

Share Services

Lets you access Sharing, Publishing, Saving and Instant Messaging capabilities.

Its content may vary depending on the current app.

Home Services

Lets you access and switch work context: your Dashboard, communities or 3DSpaces depending on the current app.

Help Services

Lets you access online user assistance, tutorials and coachmark.
