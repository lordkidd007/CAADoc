Action Bar
 

The action bar contains all app actions. It is easily accessible and discoverable.

  • General Layout
  • Flyout
  • Customizing the Action Bar

General Layout

The action bar is the main area to access app commands. Commands are organized into sections, and in groups within sections.

Sections are ordered from left to right by frequency of use or workflow.

The left part of the action bar contains commands visible in all sections (fixed area). This area contains most frequently used actions.

Sections or groups can be made up of a primary area (always visible) and a secondary area (visible on demand). The secondary area contains actions intended to be used less frequently than those of the primary area.

You can move action items to change their position in the action bar or in a flyout.

Flyout

In both primary and secondary areas, flyouts are used to combine commands that are very similar. Commands in a flyout are ordered from most frequently used (at the top) to least frequently used (at the bottom). The bottom most used command's icon is visible when the flyout is collapsed.

Customizing the Action Bar

You can reorganize actions within the action bar (move between fixed, primary and secondary areas, across or within sections as well as in the flyout).

You can also select Me > Preferences > Customize then select actions from other apps and add them to the action bar.
