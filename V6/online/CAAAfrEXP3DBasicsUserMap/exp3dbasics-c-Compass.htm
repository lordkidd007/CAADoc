Compass

The Compass is the door to all 3DEXPERIENCE Platform apps and experiences.

Compass Quadrants

It is divided in four quadrants:

  • On the north, Social and Collaborative Apps
  • On the west, 3D Modeling Apps
  • On the south, Content and Simulation Apps
  • On the east, Information Intelligence Apps.

The Play button at its center reveals available experiences and bring them to life.

Each quadrant is divided into three sections:

  • Your favorite Apps. This section is the same for all quadrants.
  • Your Apps. Sorted alphabetically.
  • The ability to learn more about the related Dassault Systèmes' brands.

To launch an app, click it or tap on it.

If a compliant content is selected prior to launching an app, this app is launched with this content.

If you have access to several platforms or if several installations are available for an app, you can select the ones to use prior to launching the app, from a drop-down list close to the app icon.

From this menu, you can also create Apps shortcuts by selecting the arrow at the end of each line.
