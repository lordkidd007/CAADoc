Deleting a Framework

This task shows you how to delete a framework from a workspace.
Before you begin:

Open the appropriate workspace.

  1. In the 3DS - Workspace Explorer, click the + sign near the root node Workspace, to expand the frameworks.

  2. Right click the framework to delete.

  3. Click Delete.

  4. A prompt opens to ask you whether you want to delete the framework.

  5. Click Yes.

    The framework is deleted.
