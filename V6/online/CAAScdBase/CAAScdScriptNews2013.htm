What's New with V6R2013 Automation
Technical Article

Abstract

This article presents Automation objects, methods, and properties that are added since V6R2012x GA to enhance V6 Automation capabilities, support functional evolutions, and correct defects. Some of those modifications may already exist in V6R2012x maintenance fixes. A few properties and methods might have been modified or removed. VB and VBScripts code using those methods may have to be modified. VB projects using the corresponding objects must be recompiled.

Consult About Automation Languages, Debug, and Compatibility for details on manual adjustments that may be required for existing VBA and VSTA projects to be usable on a new release.
