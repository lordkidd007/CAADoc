Tessellated geometry Conversion
Technical Article

Abstract

This article describes how to convert the tessellated geometry of a CAD document to V6 using the Format Conversion Services APIs.

The CATIExchangeInputDocument::GetRepresentation(“TessellatedRepresentation”) method retrieves a CATIExchangeRepresentation pointer that can be “casted” to a CATIExchangeTessellatedRepresentation
The CATIExchangeTessellatedRepresentation provides a view on the document as a tessellated representation and retrieves all the tessellated information.
