Exact geometry Conversion
Technical Article

Abstract

This article describes how to convert the exact geometry of a CAD document to V6 using the Format Conversion Services APIs.

The CATIExchangeInputDocument::GetRepresentation(?BoundaryRepresentation?) method retrieves a CATIExchangeRepresentation pointer that can be ?casted? to a CATIExchangeBoundaryRepresentation
The CATIExchangeBoundaryRepresentation provides a view on the document as a Brep and retrieves the top bodies of the representation.

Basically the interface CATIExchangeBoundaryRepresentation contains 2 methods:
EnumBodies : designed to retrieve all structured bodies containing volumes, shells, in brief 3D geometry
EnumGeometricalElements : designed to retrieve all other items, like cloud of points, coordinate systems, ?
The interface CATIExchangeBodies allows describing all entities involved in 3D geometry definition.
