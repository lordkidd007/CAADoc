Product structure Conversion
Technical Article

Abstract

This article describes how to convert the product structure of a CAD document to V6 using the Format Conversion Services APIs.

The CATIExchangeInputDocument::GetRepresentation(“ProductStructureRepresentation”) method retrieves a CATIExchangeRepresentation pointer that can be “casted” to a CATIExchangeProductRepresentation
The CATIExchangeProductRepresentation Interface is mainly dedicated to give access to root products in data exchange.
The CATIExchangeProduct and CATIExchangeProductInstance interfaces are used to expose the product structure.
