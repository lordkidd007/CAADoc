Framework Mapping

This table summarizes the mapping between the V5 frameworks and the V6 frameworks. The columns are as follows:

  • V5 Frameworks: lists the V5 frameworks.
  • Available: indicates whether the frameworks still expose classes, interfaces, global functions, etc. with V6.
  • Moved Headers: deals with headers that still exist without any modifications, but are moved to other, possibly new frameworks:
    • Mapping: gives a link to a file listing the headers moved to other frameworks, along with the names of both the V5 and V6 modules to help you update the Imakefile.mk files of your modules.
    • Frameworks: lists the frameworks where the moved headers are located with V6 to help you update the IdentityCard files of your frameworks.
  • Changes: deals with classes, interfaces, methods, or global functions that do not exist any longer. Gives a link to a file listing replaced classes, interfaces, methods, or global functions, or to an article explaining how to either replace or get rid of them in your applications.
