SketcherInterfaces

Abstract

You will find here the V6 changes compared to V5 for the framework SketcherInterfaces. Note that unchanged classes, interfaces, and global functions that are located in V6 and in V5 in the same framework are not listed here.

Changed Classes or Interfaces

This table summarizes the V5 SketcherInterfaces classes, interfaces, methods, and global functions that you may have used in your applications built on top of V5 and that either still exist as they are in V5, but elsewhere, or do not exist any longer with V6. For each of these:

  • If the class or interface still exists as in V5, but in a different framework, its V6 framework and module is given to help you change your IdentityCard and Imakefile.mk files.
  • If a one-to-one mapping exists to replacing classes, interfaces, methods, or global functions, links to these substitutes are listed and pointed to.
  • Otherwise, a V6 Adoption article is pointed to to explain how to replace or get rid of them.
