MeasurableCylinder (Object)

Interface representing the measurement on a cylinder.
Get the area, the center of gravity and the perimeter by inheritance. Get the radius, a point and the two limit points on the axis of the cylinder.

Method Index

GetAxis
Retrieves the axis vector of the cylinder.
GetPoint
Retrieves the position of a point on the axis of the cylinder.
GetPoints
Retrieves the position of the two limit points on the axis of the cylinder.
GetRadius
Retrieves the radius of the cylinder.
