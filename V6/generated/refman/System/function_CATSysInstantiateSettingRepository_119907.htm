System Global Function CATSysInstantiateSettingRepository


CATSysInstantiateSettingRepository( const iName,
oCtrl)

Parameters:
iName
The name of the CATIASettingRepository used by the applications to retrieve it. Legal values: The length of the string must be less than or equal to

Returns:
Legal values: S_OK on success, and E_FAIL otherwise
