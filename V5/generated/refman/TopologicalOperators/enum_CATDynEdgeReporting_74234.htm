TopologicalOperators Enumeration CATDynEdgeReporting

enum CATDynEdgeReporting {
  CATDynDefaultEdgeReporting,
  CATDynInternalEdgeReporting
}
Defines whether the internal ribbon edges are logged inside the journal.
Values:
CATDynDefaultEdgeReporting
The internal edges of the ribbon (such as the common edge between the ribbon and the adjacent face) are not logged inside the journal.
CATDynInternalEdgeReporting
The internal edges of the ribbon (such as the common edge between the ribbon and the adjacent face) are logged inside the journal.
