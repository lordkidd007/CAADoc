GSOInterfaces Enumeration CATGSMUnfoldTargetOrientationMode

enum CATGSMUnfoldTargetOrientationMode {
  CATGSMUnfoldTargetOrientationMode_None,
  CATGSMUnfoldTargetOrientationMode_ReverseU,
  CATGSMUnfoldTargetOrientationMode_ReverseV,
  CATGSMUnfoldTargetOrientationMode_ReverseUReverseV,
  CATGSMUnfoldTargetOrientationMode_SwapReverseU,
  CATGSMUnfoldTargetOrientationMode_SwapReverseV,
  CATGSMUnfoldTargetOrientationMode_SwapReverseUReverseV,
  CATGSMUnfoldTargetOrientationMode_Swap
}
Modes for target surface orientation.
Values:
CATGSMUnfoldTargetOrientationMode_None
no axis inversion
CATGSMUnfoldTargetOrientationMode_ReverseU
U inversion axis
CATGSMUnfoldTargetOrientationMode_ReverseV
V inversion axis
CATGSMUnfoldTargetOrientationMode_ReverseUReverseV
U inversion axis and V inversion axis
CATGSMUnfoldTargetOrientationMode_SwapReverseU
U inversion axis and swap U and V axis
CATGSMUnfoldTargetOrientationMode_SwapReverseV
V inversion axis and swap U and V axis
CATGSMUnfoldTargetOrientationMode_SwapReverseUReverseV
U inversion axis, V inversion axis and swap U and V axis
CATGSMUnfoldTargetOrientationMode_Swap
Swap U and V axis

CATIGSMUnfold, CATIGSOFactory
