public class CATPreactivate

Class to notify the preselection of the representation.
Role: This notification sent by a CATManipulator indicates that the mouse intersects the representation controlled by the manipulator. It can be received by any CATCommand class by means of the callback through the CATCommand.AddAnalyseNotificationCB method.
