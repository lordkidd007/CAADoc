Scene (Object)

IUnknown
  |
  +---IDispatch
    |
    +---CATBaseUnknown
      |
      +---CATBaseDispatch
        |
        +---AnyObject
          |
          +---Scene

Represent the scene.
A scene stores a state of a product in a given ProductDocument.

This state is composed of product properties, graphical attibutes, activation status and position for each component of the product.
