DrawingDimLine (Object)

IUnknown
  |
  +---IDispatch
    |
    +---CATBaseUnknown
      |
      +---CATBaseDispatch
        |
        +---AnyObject
          |
          +---DrawingDimLine

Manages dimension line of a dimension in drawing view.

This interface is obtained from DrawingDimension.GetDimLine method.
