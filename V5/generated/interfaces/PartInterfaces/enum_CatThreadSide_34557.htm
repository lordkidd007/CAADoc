CatThreadSide (Enumeration)

enum CatThreadSide {
  catRightSide,
  catLeftSide
}
Threading/Tapping side It describes the side of thread/tap.
Values:
catRightSide
The Thread/tap is right-threaded
catLeftSide
The Thread/tap is left-threaded
