CatPrismOrientation (Enumeration)

enum CatPrismOrientation {
  catRegularOrientation,
  catInverseOrientation
}
Prism extrusion orientation mode.
Values:
catRegularOrientation
The prism is extruded with the same orientation as the one given by its extrusion direction vector
catInverseOrientation
The prism is extruded in the reverse direction than its extrusion direction vector orientation
