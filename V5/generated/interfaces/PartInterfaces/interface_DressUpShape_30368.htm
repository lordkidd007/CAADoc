DressUpShape (Object)

IUnknown
  |
  +---IDispatch
    |
    +---CATBaseUnknown
      |
      +---CATBaseDispatch
        |
        +---AnyObject
          |
          +---Shape

Represents the shapes built with other shape sub-elements such as faces or edges.
It is the base object for chamfers, drafts, fillets, scalings, shells, and thickness objects.
