Trim (Object)

IUnknown
  |
  +---IDispatch
    |
    +---CATBaseUnknown
      |
      +---CATBaseDispatch
        |
        +---AnyObject
          |
          +---Shape
            |
            +---BooleanShape
              |
              +---Trim

Represents the Trim, or union trim boolean operation.
It is performed between a body and the current shape.
