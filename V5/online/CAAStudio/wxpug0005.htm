Explore 3DS Workspace

The Explore 3DS Workspace command opens a Windows explorer. Its contents depends on the selected node in the Workspace Explorer.
Before you begin:

Open the appropriate workspace.

  1. On the Window menu, point to 3DS Windows, and click Explore 3DS Workspace.

    A Windows Explorer opens the folder of the workspace root.
